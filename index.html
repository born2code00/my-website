Privacy Policy Last updated: 01/08/2026 This Privacy Policy explains how Heaven and Hell (“the game”, “we”, “our”) handles user information. No Account Creation The game does not require users to create an account. No registration, login, or personal profile is needed to play. No Personal Data Collection We do not directly collect, store, or share any personal data such as: - name - email address - phone number - location - contacts - photos or files Internet Usage The game may use an internet connection for basic functionality. However, this connection is not used to collect personal information from users. No Advertisements The game does not display ads and does not use advertising SDKs. Third-Party Services The game does not integrate third-party services that collect personal data (such as analytics, ads, or tracking tools). Children’s Privacy The game does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected at all, it is safe in this regard. Changes to This Policy If the game’s functionality changes in the future in a way that affects data usage, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.
